How it Works

We get it. Finding women in tech to mentor or to be mentored is tough. At Sage, we make it easy for women to connect with other women in tech. Whether you're an aspiring developer or a seasoned veteran who wants to give back, Sage gives you the ability to message and build relationships with women in tech. The main reason: it's awesome! But in cause that's not a good enough reason, we'll tell you more... (eventually)

About Us

Sage was created within 24 hours during the We Hack 2017 Hackathon sponsored and hosted by TUNE, Inc. So, who are the brains behind Sage?

Sage was created by a talented group of women from the Seattle area. On the back end team was Mikayla Jordon, Daniela Bizgu, and Yishui Liu. On the front end team was Brianna Burrows, Sereina Blanchard, and Maddy Stevens.
